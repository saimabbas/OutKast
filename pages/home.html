ABOUT THE GAME LORE

The Rise of the Durasha Federation

Once upon a time, eons ago, the different kingdoms, free cities and provinces of the continent were all independent. As civilizations grew and prospered, others would crumble and fall, and there was a certain harmony in the world. Nation fought against nation while maintaining embassies in others and growing trade with another but these times have come to an end.

From the South an enemy to relative peace stood up, effectively disrupting the harmony of the last centuries. They called themselves the Durasha Federation and were a loosely arranged group of states united under one banner with at the head the Emperor. Each nation was allowed their own form of government but were restricted in their policies by those of the Empire.

The taxes they had to pay and the soldiers they had to provide to the massive army of the Durasha Federation kept the possibility for revolts very low as well. And thus, government after government, nation after nation toppled over and their heads were replaced by loyalists to the Empire. It only felt like a matter of time before the whole continent would bend their knee to their overlord, the Emperor.

The Values of the Durasha Federation

The Durasha federation took a double stance on their conquered territories. Everyone was free to move and migrate within the Empire but there was an effective classicist element within the Durasha Federation. The native races of Durasha, the blue skinned water dragon race, the Naga, and the red skinned fire dragon race, the Aidan, were by law above all other citizens.

Other ethnicities were banned from climbing up the ranks of the military to the highest ranks, property disputes were always settled in favor of the Naga and the Aidan and even members of the two races who were taking part of interracial relations were shunned by their own community. This has become a problem in the last decades, with the Naga and Aidan effectively hunting down half bloods to keep the blood of their people pure.

Officially however, the Durasha Federation stated their position as one of self-dependency, giving conquered nations their own rights to uphold the law in their own territory. Kings who willingly bend the knee were reinstated after the conquest, others who put up a fight were replaced by a more willing family member or other noble family.

The Regions of the Durasha Federation

Once upon a time, eons ago, the different kingdoms, free cities and provinces of the continent were all independent. As civilizations grew and prospered, others would crumble and fall, and there was a certain harmony in the world. Nation fought against nation while maintaining embassies in others and growing trade with another but these times have come to an end.

As the conquests of the Durasha Federation raged on, there was no one official map to the Durasha Federation because of the ever-changing nature of the regions. There are however a few key regions that can be described.

Durashi: home of the Naga and the Aidan

The native lands of the Naga and the Aidan, a rugged land split through by an inhospitable mountain range. It is widely believed that both of them come from one and the same race but at some point in their evolution they were split up. The Naga in their homeland were mostly confined to the cliffs on the coast, carving out entire cities in it while the Aidan built their settlements right in the small crevasses and canyons of the mountains. Over time, the Naga due to their diet of mostly seafood got softer, almost like fish-scaled skin while the Aidan became more rugged and brownish red. Now this region is a more traditionally and ceremonially used place, with no real cities or population.

The Homelands: Land of the metropole

What the Naga and the Aidan refer to as their Homelands is in fact their first jointly conquered territories, the lands that were inhabited by the now extinct people, the Sha’hal. The lands were lush, filled with jungles and hills filled with useful mining resources and so became an ideal target for their expansion. their armies united and overwhelmed the natives, effectively killing all of them.

In hindsight they regretted their slaughter and instituted the idea of self-dependency if they ever conquered another territory. As they now had more land to rule over, the Durasha Federation was installed, with a council of equal amount Aidan & Naga, with an Emperor presiding the Council of the Dragon. They realised they needed more labour forces to work the mining operations they needed, the forestry for their construction and in general servants to live like kings themselves.

The regions closest to them fell victim to their expansion drift and became part of what is now known as the Homelands. In the shadow of the old mountains, there has been a city constructed that has no equal in the world. The Capital of the Homelands and the Durasha Federation in general,is always building up, attracting more people, Naga, Aidan and others alike. It became known as Mogla Durash.
